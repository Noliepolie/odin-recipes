Granola Yogurt

This is an easy recipe for super healthy homemade granola. A tasty mix of nuts, seeds, oats and dried fruits with a hint of coconut and honey.

Coconut oil - coconut oil can be either solid or liquid. This depends on the temperature in the room you keep it in. If your coconut oil is solid, melt it first and then measure ⅓ cup

The baking time may vary slightly depending on your oven so keep an eye on it when making it for the first time

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rolled Oats (200g)
  • A pinch of Salt
  • ½ cup Dried Figs (50g)
  • ½ cup Hazelnuts (40g)
  • ½ cup Pitted Dried Dates (40g)
  • ⅓ cup Whole Almonds (40g)
  • 2 tbsp Desiccated Coconut
  • ⅓ cup Coconut Oil (40ml)
  • 5 tbsp Honey
  • 4 tbsp Chia Seeds
  • ⅓ cup Goji Berries (20g)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F/150°C
  2. Meanwhile: In a large bowl, combine the oats, salt, and roughly chopped figs, hazelnuts, dates and almonds. Add coconut and mix everything until well combined
  3. Pour in the coconut oil and mix until all the ingredients are well coated in it. At last add honey and stir
  4. Transfer onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper, spread the mixture around and transfer to the oven
  5. Bake at 300°F/150°C for 15-20 minutes. Stir 2 or 3 times during baking so that everything bakes evenly and the top doesn't get burnt
  6. When ready, take it out from the oven and stir in chia seeds and goji berries
  7. Let it cool completely before transferring it into an air-tight container
  8. Enjoy with milk, yogurt or on its own
